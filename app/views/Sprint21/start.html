Check agricultural scheme payments to farmers and land managers in England.

Use this service to view payments from the:

  • Sustainable Farming Incentive pilot
  • Farming Equipment and Technology Fund
This service only shows payments received between 1 April 2021 and 31 March 2022.
What data we publish

The data we publish varies for different schemes and depends on the total funding a business has received.

Payment data will remain online for 3 years from the date we publish it.

Sustainable Farming Incentive and the Farming Equipment and Technology Fund

When a business has received total agricultural scheme payments of £1,250 or more, we publish:

  • full names of agreement holders and businesses
  • partial postcodes, post towns and districts where the agreement holder is resident or located
  • total payments received by the agreement holder in the lastfinancial year
  • a description of the activities financed by the relevant payments
